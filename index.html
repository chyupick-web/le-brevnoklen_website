What is Vyral

Vyral Smart Contracts power a decentralized advertising ecosystem of blockchain incentive programs from fueling viral growth for crypto token sales to tokenizing reward programs for businesses.

Vyral Nodes generate SHARE tokens via action-based minting for every referral connected to the Vyral Reward Chain.

Vyral Nodes

Once an ether address joins the Vyral Referral Chain & it is cryptographically proven on the Ethereum blockchain the address receives SHARE tokens & transforms into a Vyral Node.

Each Vyral Node can earn between
7% & 33% depending on the size of it's Vyral Tree -- referrals are an easy process via Vyral's DAPP.

Share Rewards

Refer someone via the first ever decentralized reward system & earn up to 33% of the resulting contribution in SHARE tokens.
